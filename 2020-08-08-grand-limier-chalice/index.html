The Grand Limier’s chalice

Posted by Les Francs Limiers on 8/8/2020

A peculiar object, which was transmitted from generation to generation among the (quite) secret society of the Francs Limiers, a forgotten academic society from Lorraine, France, created in 1750.

It is told that this chalice has been used in many vigils and great events during the Francs Limiers’s time, especially during meetings with their counterparts from Metz and Nancy, but also with the ones from Lyon and Paris.

Some phenomenons have been orally reported regarding the signs one would have seen at the bottom of this chalice. Sometimes a face, sometimes names.

Fate would have it that these events did occur at a precise date, making this item even more mystical.
